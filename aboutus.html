What is a Disease Radar?

Disease radars are systems and tools used to monitor, detect, and predict the spread of diseases, particularly infectious diseases. They combine data collection, surveillance, and analysis to provide early warnings for potential outbreaks, track ongoing epidemics, and guide public health responses. These systems can involve a range of technologies, including traditional reporting, digital tools, and advanced analytics.
